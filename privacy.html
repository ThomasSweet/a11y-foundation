Privacy

The short version: this site collects nothing beyond what a web server needs to deliver pages. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking.

No tracking

There are no analytics, advertising, cookies, fingerprinting, or third-party embeds. Your operating-system preferences (dark mode, reduced motion, increased contrast) are read by your own browser to style the page and are never transmitted or stored anywhere.

Server logs

Like most websites, the hosting provider may automatically record technical access data — IP address, date and time, the file requested, and your browser's user-agent string — to deliver the site and keep it secure. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating the site reliably and securely). [Hosting provider — to be confirmed] processes this data on our behalf.
